Laser Engraver

Our epilog laser engraver uses picture files to physically ingrave onto material. Materials that have been used, are wood, and acrylic, there are plenty of other materials that could be tested.

Vinyl Cutter

The Roland Vinyl Cutter uses picture outlines to cut details into vinyl sheets, which can be cut out, and placed anywhere as a sticker or decoration. The Vinyl Cutter runs on a program called CutStudio, which traces the outline of pictures and creates a vector image to print.

Saws

The FabLab has a large arrangement of tools, including saws. It has plenty of handsaws, as well as a band and chop saw.

CNC Router

The CNC router uses MasterCam files to cut out grooves and edges out of wood and perhaps other materials.

CNC Mill

The CNC Mill uses a sort of drill bit to cut out of materials at incredibly fast speeds, it is a smaller contained version of the router.
